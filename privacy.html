Privacy Policy

App: Paw Pick — Dog Pick · Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Paw Pick ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the app. We built Paw Pick as a simple, cozy puzzle game, and we keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the app and show ads that keep it free.

Information We Collect

The app itself does not ask you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email, or address. However, to display advertising and measure basic performance, our third‑party advertising partner (Google AdMob) may automatically collect:

  • Identifiers — device advertising identifier (IDFA/IDFV) and similar device IDs.
  • Usage Data — interactions with ads and general app activity.
  • Device Information — device model, operating system version, and language.
  • Approximate (Coarse) Location — derived from your IP address, used to serve region‑appropriate ads.
  • Diagnostics — crash and performance data.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the game.
  • To display advertisements (including personalized ads where permitted).
  • To measure ad performance and prevent fraud/abuse.

Advertising & Third Parties

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. Google may collect and process data as described in Google's Privacy Policy and its advertising policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from partner apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Tracking & Your Choices (App Tracking Transparency)

On iOS, the app asks for your permission before tracking you across apps and websites (Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt). You can decline, and the app will continue to work with non‑personalized ads. You can change this any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. You may also reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

Paw Pick is rated 4+ and is intended for a general audience. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app shows ads, it is not designated as a "Made for Kids" app.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's own retention policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
